## 🔧 What quant-signal does

quant-signal helps you check crypto signals with live market data.

It brings together:
- Real-time Kraken price data
- TradingView charts
- Gemini AI analysis
- A signal pipeline that can filter and score signals
- A modern web app view for reading market changes

Use it when you want a clear view of a trade idea before you act on it.

Choose the coin or pair you want to track, such as BTC/USD or ETH/USD.

### 2. Read the chart
Look at the TradingView chart to see recent price movement, trend, and support or resistance areas.

### 3. Check the signal
Review the signal output from the pipeline. This may show a strong buy, weak buy, hold, or other status.

### 4. Read the AI analysis

### 5. Decide what to do
Use the signal as one input. Check the chart and price action before making a move.

## ⚙️ Key parts of the app

- **Live Kraken data**: Pulls current market data for crypto pairs
- **TradingView charts**: Shows price history and chart tools
- **Gemini AI analysis**: Adds a text review of signal quality
- **Signal pipeline**: Filters and scores trade ideas
- **React interface**: Gives the app a clean screen for day-to-day use
- **PostgreSQL support**: Stores app data and signal records
- **Drizzle ORM**: Helps the app read and write data in a safe way
- **Server-sent events**: Updates the screen with new market data without refresh
